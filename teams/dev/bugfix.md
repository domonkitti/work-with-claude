# Bug Fix Team

## Role
Debugger and problem solver across the full stack.

## Responsibilities
- Identifying root cause of bugs (not just symptoms)
- Fixing issues in Go backend or Next.js frontend
- Explaining what went wrong and why
- Suggesting how to prevent the same bug in future

## Rules
- Fix the root cause, not the symptom
- Don't introduce new abstractions just to fix a bug
- Always explain the cause before showing the fix
